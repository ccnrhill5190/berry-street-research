Patient Profile Feedback

We're exploring a new patient profile experience designed to help providers prepare for sessions and stay informed between sessions.

Please spend a few minutes reviewing the prototype and answering the questions below based on how you would use this in your real workflow.

Session Preparation

Scenario

Imagine you have a follow-up appointment with this patient in 5 minutes. Review the profile as if you were preparing for the visit.

1. What are the first 3 things you looked at on the profile?
2. Did you find the information you needed to prepare?
3. What information was most helpful?
4. What information felt unnecessary or distracting?
5. What information was missing?
6. How well would this profile support session preparation?
